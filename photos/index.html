As an adult, I continued to travel with a camera to make my own memories. I am photography beginner using a Canon 100D camera (with a Canon EF-S 18-55 STM lens). The latest countries I have visited are the U.S.A. (New York / Boston / Providence), Indonesia (Bali & Gili Islands), Corsica (GR20) and Portugal. I now live in Toronto (Canada).
